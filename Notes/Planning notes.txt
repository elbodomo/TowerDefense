1. Core Architecture (Inheritance & Systems)
Enemy System:

EnemyBase (Abstract): Handles currentHealth, moveSpeed, damageToBase, and OnDeath events.

Scout: High speed, low health.

Tank: Low speed, high health.

Tower System:

TowerBase (Abstract): Handles LayerMask sensing, fireRate cooldowns, and range.

Sniper: Uses Physics.OverlapSphere to find the nearest target; applies damage instantly to one unit.

AOE: Uses Physics.OverlapSphere to apply damage to all units within radius.

Support: Generates currency on a set interval.

Currency & Health:

Currency: Nails (Primary resource for building/upgrading).

Health: Base Wall Integrity (Total HP of the player's base).

2. Technical Implementation
Object Pooling: Mandatory for Scout and Tank prefabs to optimize WebGL performance.

Combat Feedback: Sniper triggers a MuzzleFlash particle on the tower; Enemies trigger a Damage particle system on their own transform when hit.

Placement: 1x1 unit grid snapping using Mathf.RoundToInt. Raycast validation ensures towers are placed on "Ground" and not "Path" layers.

Navigation: Waypoint-based movement. Vehicles move towards the next index and rotate to face the direction of travel.

3. Game Flow
Spawn: Enemy Spawner at start of path.

Goal: Player Base at end of path.

UI: * Nail Counter (Currency).

Wall Integrity (Base Health).

Wave Control (Start Button).

Tower Shop (Selection).

4. Optional Polish (Final Stage)

VFX: Enhancing particle systems and environment details.

3D Assets: Adding low-poly environmental models.
